Displacement

Displacement is the shortest distance covered between two points. Displacement can be caused by many factors, including forces acting on the object. When an object is displaced, it gains velocity and its new position is usually different from its original position. It is denoted by m. It is a vector quantity.

Formula

2as= v2 - u2
